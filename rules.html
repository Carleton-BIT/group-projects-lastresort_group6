Carleton University Gym Rules

1. All members must scan their student ID and reserve a time before entering the gym.
2. Proper gym attire is required at all times (athletic clothing and clean indoor shoes).
3. Wipe down all equipment after use.
4. Return weights and equipment to their proper place after use.
5. No food is allowed in the workout areas. Water bottles are permitted.
6. Be respectful to staff and other gym users at all times.
7. Do not drop weights unnecessarily or create excessive noise.
8. Use equipment only for its intended purpose.
9. Report any injuries or damaged equipment to staff immediately.
10. Follow all posted safety guidelines and staff instructions.
